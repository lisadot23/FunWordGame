FunWordGame

How to Play

Guess each 5-letter word in 6 tries or fewer.

A
Green = correct letter, correct spot
B
Yellow = correct letter, wrong spot
C
Gray = letter not in the word

Use the clues from each guess to find the answer.

Daily Play

Play up to 10 words each day (adjustable in Settings).

Everyone gets the same words on the same date.

Your progress, stats, and streaks are saved in your browser on this device.

Theme Days

Some holidays and special events use themed word lists.

Theme answers may include names, characters, places, acronyms, or other theme-related words.

Feedback

Found a bug or have an idea for a future theme? Use the ✉ button to send feedback.
